lg: 0.5rem xl: 0.75rem full: 9999px spacing: unit: 8px container-max: 1280px gutter: 24px margin-page: 48px section-padding: 120px --- ## Brand & Style The design system is engineered for the high-end technology professional. It conveys precision, technical mastery, and forward-thinking innovation. The brand personality is "Expert-Technical," balancing a rigorous, systematic layout with the atmospheric depth of modern software interfaces. The aesthetic combines **Minimalism** with **Subtle Glassmorphism**. This approach ensures that content remains the primary focus while using translucent layers and blurred backgrounds to create a sense of physical space and sophisticated "dark mode" depth. The result is a UI that feels like a premium developer tool—functional, fast, and high-fidelity. ## Colors The palette is rooted in a deep charcoal-navy base to reduce eye strain and provide a canvas for high-energy accents. - **Base (#090b10):** Used for the primary background to establish a solid, "bottomless" depth. - **Vibrant Emerald (#38d996):** The primary action color. It signifies success, interactivity, and "go" signals. - **Electric Blue (#64b5ff):** A highlight accent used for information callouts, links, and secondary visual interest. - **Glass Surfaces:** Semi-transparent variations of the background (approx. 60% opacity) are used for panels, allowing background glows or content to subtly bleed through. ## Typography The typography system utilizes **Inter** for its exceptional legibility and neutral, systematic character. To inject a "tech" feel, **Space Grotesk** is introduced for small labels and utility text, providing a geometric, monospaced-adjacent aesthetic. Hierarchy is established through extreme weight contrast. Large headlines use "Extra Bold" or "Black" weights with tight letter spacing to create high-impact anchors for the eye. Body text is kept spacious with a 1.6 line-height to ensure readability against the dark background. ## Layout & Spacing This design system employs a **Fixed Grid** model for desktop, centered within the viewport to maintain focus. The layout relies on an 8px spacing scale, ensuring all elements align to a consistent rhythm. Generous whitespace is a core requirement. Vertical section padding is intentionally large (120px+) to allow each portfolio piece or skill set to "breathe" and stand as a distinct unit. Use a 12-column grid for project galleries, allowing items to span 4 columns (3-up) or 6 columns (2-up). ## Elevation & Depth Depth is created through **Glassmorphism** rather than traditional drop shadows. 1. **Level 0 (Base):** The #090b10 background. 2. **Level 1 (Panels):** A slightly lighter surface (#0d1117) with a 1px border at 8% white opacity. 3. **Level 2 (Glass):** Floating elements use a background-blur (20px to 40px) with a semi-transparent fill. 4. **Accents:** "Light leaks" or soft radial gradients of Electric Blue or Emerald Green are placed *behind* the glass layers to create a sense of glowing energy emanating from within the interface. ## Shapes The shape language is "Soft-Technical." Elements use a 0.25rem (4px) base radius to maintain a precise, engineered feel without being sharp or aggressive. Large cards and sections utilize `rounded-lg` (8px) to soften the overall container, while buttons and input fields remain at the base 4px for a tighter, tool-like appearance. ## Components ### Buttons - **Primary:** Solid Emerald Green (#38d996) with black text. No shadows; use a slight "glow" (outer shadow of the same color at low opacity) on hover. - **Secondary:** Ghost style. 1px border in Electric Blue (#64b5ff) with matching text. - **Interaction:** On hover, primary buttons should shift upwards by 2px and increase glow intensity. ### Technical Skill Chips Small, 4px rounded containers. Background is a very dark blue/grey with a 1px Electric Blue border at 20% opacity. Text uses the **label-caps** typography style. ### Project Cards Cards use the Level 2 Glassmorphism style. - **Default State:** 1px subtle border, 16px blur. - **Hover State:** Border color transitions to Emerald Green, and a soft Emerald inner-glow appears. The featured image should slightly scale up (1.05x). ### Hero Sections High-contrast typography against the dark base. Use a large, blurred background orb of Electric Blue to create a focal point behind the main headline. ### Input Fields Dark backgrounds (#050505) with 1px borders. Focus state uses a 1px Emerald Green border and a 2px outer glow. Labels use the **label-caps** style for a "dashboard" aesthetic.
